🍗 Crispy Fried Chicken

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 🍗 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 🥛 1 cup buttermilk
  • 🍚 2 cups flour
  • 🧂 1 tbsp salt & 1 tsp black pepper
  • 🌶️ 1 tbsp paprika
  • 🥚 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 🛢️ 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. 🍗 Marinate chicken in buttermilk and let it sit for 1 hour.
  2. 🥣 In a bowl, mix flour, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. 🥚 Dip the chicken in beaten eggs, then coat with the flour mix.
  4. 🔥 Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry chicken for 8-10 minutes until golden brown.
  5. 🧻 Place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. 🍽️ Serve hot with your favorite dip!
