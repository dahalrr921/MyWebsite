🌽 Spicy Corn Delight

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 🌽 2 cups sweet corn (boiled)
  • 🧈 1 tbsp butter
  • 🌶️ 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 🧂 Salt to taste
  • 🧄 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 🌿 Fresh coriander for garnish

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. 🍳 Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. 🌽 Add boiled corn and sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. 🌶️ Sprinkle chili flakes, garlic powder, and salt.
  4. 🍋 Squeeze fresh lemon juice and mix well.
  5. 🌿 Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot!
